## Privacy Policy
**Last updated: March 31, 2026**

### Overview
This Privacy Policy describes our policies and procedures on the collection, <br />
use and disclosure of your information when you use our mobile application.

### Data Collection and Usage
Rembirth does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or information of any kind.
- **No Accounts:** We do not require you to create an account.
- **No Personal Info:** We do not collect your name, email address, or phone number.
- **No Tracking:** We do not use any third-party SDKs, analytics, or tracking software.

### Local Storage Only
All data created or entered within the App is stored **locally on your device only**.
- **No Cloud:** This data is never uploaded to any external servers or cloud services.
- **No Access:** We have no access to the data you enter.
- **Data Retention:** Because your data is stored locally, it will be permanently deleted if you uninstall the App from your device.

### Device Permissions
To function properly, the App may request the following permissions:
- **Notifications:** Used solely to send you reminders for upcoming birthdays. <br />
  These notifications are generated entirely on your device. No data is sent to a server to trigger them.

### Children's Privacy
Our App does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. <br />
We comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar global regulations.

### Security
The security of your data relies on the security of your hardware. We recommend using your device’s built-in security <br />
features (Passcode, FaceID, or TouchID) to protect the information stored on your phone. <br />
We recommend keeping your device software updated to ensure the best security for your local data.

### Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Since we do not collect user contact information,  <br />
we encourage you to review this page periodically for any changes. <br />
Changes are effective immediately upon posting.
